# voxyl-community

Welcome to the official support hub for Voxyl, the social podcast playlist aggregator.

Voxyl is designed to give you total control over your audio experience by allowing you to curate, share, and enjoy custom podcast streams from your favorite RSS feeds.

🛠 How to Get Support
If you are experiencing technical issues, found a bug, or have a feature suggestion, please use one of the methods below:

1. Open a GitHub Issue (Recommended)
The best way to track bugs is by opening an "Issue" right here in this repository.

Click on the Issues tab at the top.

Click "New Issue".

Describe the problem and, if possible, include your device model (e.g., iPhone 15) and iOS version.

🔐 Privacy & Terms
Your privacy is a priority. Voxyl only collects the minimum data required for app functionality (such as name and email for account creation).

Privacy Policy: https://voxyl-app.base44.app/privacy

Account Deletion: Users can delete their accounts and all associated data directly within the app's Profile Settings.

📍 About the Project
